About AFA

Afro Film Alliance is more than just a film initiative; it's a movement. Born from the desire to unite African filmmakers, AFA seeks to provide a platform for profound and impactful African-inspired stories.

Through cross-national and cross-continental collaborations, we challenge stereotypes and exhibit the vibrant tapestry of African rich cultural heritage.
